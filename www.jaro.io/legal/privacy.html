Privacy Policy

July 2021

I value your privacy as much as my own, so I'm committed to keeping your personal and business information safe. I don't feel good about how privacy and tracking is often handled in today's world, so I only ask for the bare minimum.

01

General

01.01

Personal Information

When you use the contact form on this Site, I collect certain information from you, including your name, company, email address, phone number and more.  I refer to this information as personal Information.

When I collect your personal information

I only store your personal information if you choose to submit the contact form on this site and agree to the privacy policy.

Additionally, if you fill out the form and don't submit it, your entries are stored locally on your own device so that they're still here when you reload the page. However, this data is deleted automatically as soon as the browser session ends and no one but you has access to it.

What I use your personal information for

I use the personal Information that I collect to talk about your project offer, so only for the reason why you filled out the form anyway.

How long I store your personal information

I will store your personal information unless and until you ask me to delete it.

Who i share your personal information with

I never share your personal information with anyone else.

01.02

Device Information

When you visit the Site, I automatically collect certain information about your device, including information about your operating system, web browser, screen size and location. Additionally, as you browse the Site, I collect information about the individual web pages that you view, what websites or search terms brought you to the Site, and information about how you interact with it. All this data is collected anonymously and I refer to it as Device Information.

When I collect your device information

I only store your device information if you allow the use of analytics cookies in the privacy settings. In addition, all this data is collected anonymously and doesn't allow any identification of your person.

How I use your Device Information

I use the Device Information that I collect to improve and optimise my Site by generating analytics about how visitors browse and interact with it.

How long I store your personal information

I will store your device information for up to 14 months.

Who i share your personal information with

The anonymous device information is collected using Google Analytics and is therefore shared with that service as well.

01.03

Techniques

Cookies

Cookies are used to recognise a user when they revisit a website. Technically speaking, they are small files stored on your device that contain a unique anonymous identifier.

Local storage & Session storage

Local storage is used to remember user choices, such as privacy settings, when they return to a website.

Session storage is used to remember user inputs, such as form entries, when a website is reloaded. Unlike local storage, it's automatically deleted as soon as the browser session ends.

SSL or TLS encryption

To protect the transfer of confidential information, my website uses SSL or TLS encryption. This way, data sent through this website cannot be read by others.

02

Tools

02.01

Google Analytics

This site uses features of the Google Analytics web service by Google. The tool collects device information and upon consent, also sets cookies.

Google complies with the European data protection law (GDPR) and is certified under the Privacy Shield agreement. For full compliance, I have entered into a Data Processing Agreement (DPA) with Google as well.

IP anonymisation

Within the member states of the EU, IP addresses are shortened. This way, individual persons cannot be clearly identified.

03

Hosting

03.01

General

This site is hosted by the external service provider Webflow (hoster). Personal data that is collected here is therefore stored on the hoster's servers.

03.02

CDN

This site is hosted via the Content Delivery Network (CDN) of Webflow. The CDN provides website duplicates on various servers worldwide. This results in faster loading times, less downtime and better protection.

03.03

Data transfer to the USA

As this website uses tools from companies based in the USA, your personal data may therefore be passed on to US servers. And due to local laws, US authorities (e.g. intelligence agencies) might process, evaluate and permanently store this data for monitoring purposes. Unfortunately, I have no influence on these processing activities.

04

Your rights

04.01

Changes

If you are a European resident, you have the right to access all personal information I hold about you and to ask that it may be corrected, updated, or deleted.

04.02

Deletion

Of course, I can only delete your data if there are no legal obligations to retain it. In this particular case, data processing is only limited, meaning the data is blocked and no longer used for any other purpose.

04.03

Objection

You can always use your right of objection and refuse the processing of your personal data.

04.04

Customisation

You can customise and reverse personal privacy settings that have already been saved by clicking here.

05

Final

05.01

Updates

I may update this privacy policy from time to time in order to reflect, for example, changes to my practices or for other operational, legal or regulatory reasons.

05.02

Contact

For more information about my privacy practices, if you would like to make changes to your data, or if you would like to make a complaint, please contact me by E-mail or by mail using the contact details at the end of this page.
